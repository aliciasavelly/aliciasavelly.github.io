Mutter Paneer

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Below is the recipe for a delicious Indian dish that anyone will enjoy! The fresher the ingredients, the better!

Ingredients

  • Jeera seeds
  • Jeera powder
  • Dhania
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Mutter
  • Tomatoes
  • Paneer
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Cream

Directions

  1. Cook jeera seeds with a little bit of oil until they pop.
  2. Saute onions with jeera seeds and garlic. Puree onions and garlic for a smooth texture.
  3. To a larger pot, add chopped tomatoes and paneer.
  4. Add in spices, as well as the onion mixture.
  5. Add a dash of salt.
  6. Season to taste.
  7. Cut up paneer and add once peas and tomatoes are cooked through.
  8. Add cream.
